About Miller Roofing

Miller Roofing is a sample roofing company website created as part of a capstone project. The project focuses on building a roofing estimate and proposal generation system that helps organize job details, compare Good/Better/Best roofing packages, and generate customer-ready documents.

Services

Roof Replacement

Complete roof replacement using GAF roofing systems.

Roof Repairs

Repair services for leaks, flashing, vents, and damaged shingles.

Siding & Exterior Work

Exterior home improvement services including siding and trim work.

Estimate & Proposal System

The Miller Roofing Estimator is a custom-built application that collects project information such as roof pitch, total squares, eaves, rakes, ridge vent, accessories, and selected package level.

The system then compares Good, Better, and Best roofing options, calculates pricing and profit margin, and generates a Word proposal and CSV estimate export.
